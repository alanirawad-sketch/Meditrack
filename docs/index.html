Meditrack

Simple. Private. Automated. Medicine tracking from your CLI.

The Problem

Managing daily medications and supplements is a burden. Traditional apps are bloated, require accounts, and track your data. Meditrack solves this by providing a lightweight, offline-first automation tool for your terminal.

Target Audience

  • Developers: Track health without leaving the terminal.
  • Privacy Seekers: 100% local JSON storage.
  • Minimalists: No GUI, no distractions.

Monetization
